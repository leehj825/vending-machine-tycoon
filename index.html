About the Game

Vending Machine Tycoon is a strategic business simulation game where you build and manage your own vending machine empire. Start with a small investment and grow your business by strategically placing machines, managing inventory, optimizing delivery routes, and maximizing profits.

How to Play

  1. Start Your Empire: Begin with $2,000 and place your first vending machine in a strategic location.
  2. Stock Your Machines: Purchase products from the market and load them onto your trucks.
  3. Plan Routes: Create efficient delivery routes to restock your machines and maximize profits.
  4. Monitor Performance: Keep an eye on cash flow, reputation, and machine inventory levels.
  5. Expand: Use your profits to buy more machines and trucks, expanding your empire across the city.
  6. Marketing Blitz: Use the Marketing Blitz feature to trigger Rush Hour events and boost sales!
